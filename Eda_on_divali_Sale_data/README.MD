# 🪔 Diwali Sales Analysis using Python

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a Diwali Sales dataset to understand customer purchasing behavior during the festive season. The analysis helps identify key customer segments, high-performing product categories, and sales trends that can support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The main objectives of this project are:

- Analyze customer demographics and purchasing patterns.
- Identify the highest revenue-generating customer segments.
- Discover top-performing states, occupations, and product categories.
- Extract actionable business insights using data visualization.

## 📊 Key Insights

### 👩 Gender Analysis
- Female customers contribute significantly more revenue than male customers.

### 🎂 Age Group Analysis
- Customers aged **26–35 years** generate the highest sales.

### 💍 Marital Status Analysis
- Certain marital groups contribute more to overall sales, indicating targeted marketing opportunities.

### 🏙️ State Analysis
- A few states contribute a major share of total revenue and can be prioritized for promotional campaigns.

### 💼 Occupation Analysis
- Customers from specific professions generate higher sales compared to others.

### 🛍️ Product Category Analysis
- Top-selling product categories account for a significant portion of total revenue.

### 📈 Correlation Analysis
- Numerical variables show weak linear relationships, suggesting customer behavior is better explained by demographic and categorical features.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. Focus marketing campaigns on customers aged **26–35 years**.
2. Design targeted promotions for female customers.
3. Prioritize high-performing states during festive sales events.
4. Increase promotions for top-selling product categories.
5. Create personalized offers for high-spending customer segments.
